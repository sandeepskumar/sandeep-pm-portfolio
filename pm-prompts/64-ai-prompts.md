Sourced from the *64 AI Prompts for Product Managers* eBook by Pragmatic Institute. Organized by business objective to help you connect generative AI to what stakeholders care about most.

> **Tip:** Start with a broad question to set context, then iterate. The real insights emerge as the conversation deepens.

## 🏷️ Business Objective 5: Expanding to New Markets

*Use LLMs to explore potential new markets and audiences that could be addressed with your capabilities and products.*

51. What are some emerging trends or industries that could benefit from our `[product/service]`?
52. How might our `[product/service]` be adapted to meet the needs of a different demographic or customer segment?
53. What geographical regions or countries could potentially benefit from our `[product/service]`, and how would we tailor it for those markets?
54. Are there any adjacent markets or industries where our `[expertise]`, `[capabilities]` or `[product/service]` could be leveraged?
55. Can we identify any niche markets that are underserved or have unique needs that align with our `[product/service]` strengths?
56. Are there any complementary products or services to our existing `[product/service]` that we could integrate with to access new markets?
57. How can we utilize new distribution channels or partnerships to reach untapped customer segments for our `[product/service]`?
58. What cultural, regulatory, or technological differences might we encounter when entering a new market for our `[product/service]`, and how can we navigate them?
59. Can we conduct market research and surveys to identify potential customer segments and their specific needs?
60. How can we modify pricing models, packaging, or positioning to appeal to different market segments?
61. What marketing and promotional strategies should be adopted to gain traction in a new market?
62. Are there any global events or changes that might create opportunities for our product/service in new markets?
63. Could our `[product/service]` be used for a different purpose or industry than originally intended?
64. What are the biggest barriers to entry in new markets for our `[product/service]`, and how can we overcome them?
